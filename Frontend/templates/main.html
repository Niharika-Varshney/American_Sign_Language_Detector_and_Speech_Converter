About the Project

This project aims to bridge the gap between speech-impaired and visually impaired individuals by using AI-powered technology to convert sign language gestures into text and speech. It recognizes and translates sign language gestures into text in real-time and also converts them into speech. The system processes gestures one by one, joining them to form complete words or sentences, allowing for smooth communication.

How It Works

  • Capture your sign language gesture using a webcam.
  • The model processes the gesture to detect and recognize the sign.
  • The recognized gesture is translated into text and speech.
